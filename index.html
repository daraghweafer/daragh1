Greg is the Director of the Ross School of Business and the head of Business Education and Financial Literacy at John F. Ross C.V.I. located in Guelph, Ontario. In this role, Greg has pushed financial literacy concepts to the forefront of the program creating personal finance lessons that are geared toward teens and young adults.

Outside the classroom, Greg has written numerous columns in the Guelph Mercury Tribune and most recently developed – a series of Teen Money Tips that provide financial tips and advice for young adults. Greg has spoken at seminars, including the Chartered Professional Accountants of Ontario – No Limits Conference, where he highlighted the series and its classroom applications.
